This is Lindenhof in Switzerland

Moving on to the next city, we will take everyone to visit the old town of Zurich. Everyone will find a viewpoint where you can see a beautiful view of Zurich from a high angle, which is Lindenhof. It is a public park located on a hill. It can be called a place to relax, eat, enjoy the breeze, view, take pictures to the fullest. You will also see the beauty of vintage architecture that is worth looking at and exploring. Anyone who wants to visit Switzerland in a slow life, not crowded, and quiet must come to visit Lindenhof.

This is Jiuzhaigou in China

Jiuzhaigou is a park full of many trees, surrounded by lakes and waterfalls. If you come in the autumn when the trees are shedding their leaves, you will see the colors of the valley that vary in gradation. It is so beautiful that it looks like heaven on earth. If anyone has the opportunity to travel to China, they should go at least once.
